About the Conference

The International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR) is a premier gathering of researchers and practitioners dealing with learning representations within the context of machine learning. It aims to foster collaborations and highlight state-of-the-art approaches in the field.

Conference Venue: San Francisco Conference Center

The conference will be held at the San Francisco Conference Center, located at 123 Conference Center Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105. This modern venue accommodates approximately 1,000 participants and includes high-speed internet, state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment, and ample breakout rooms. Nearby attractions include the iconic Ferry Building and Market Street. For those driving, on-site parking is available, and convenient public transport options can connect you with the venue.
